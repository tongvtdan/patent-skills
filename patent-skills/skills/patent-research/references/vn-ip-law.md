# Vietnamese IP Law — Key Patent Provisions

*Reference for: Luật Sở hữu trí tuệ (IP Law) No. 50/2005/QH11, amended by Law No. 36/2009/QH12, Law No. 07/2022/QH15 (effective Jan 1, 2023)*

---

## Part 1: Patentable Subject Matter

### Article 58 — Protectable Inventions
Patents protect inventions that are:
- Technical solutions (sản phẩm hoặc quy trình)
- In the form of a product, process, or use

**Product**: Device, substance, microorganism, plant/animal cell, etc.
**Process**: Method of manufacturing, treatment, testing

### Article 59 — Non-Patentable Subject Matter
The following are NOT patentable:
1. Scientific discoveries, theories, mathematical methods
2. Mental acts, games, business methods
3. **Computer programs** (but: software *embedded in a device* or with *technical character* may qualify as a technical invention)
4. Aesthetic design solutions → use Industrial Design protection
5. Plant varieties, animal breeds → use Plant Variety Rights
6. Processes of essentially biological nature for producing plants/animals
7. Human/animal disease prevention or treatment by therapy/surgery
8. Inventions contrary to social ethics, public order, human health, environment

### Article 60 — Novelty
An invention is novel if it:
- Has not been publicly disclosed anywhere in the world
- Before the filing date (or priority date under Paris Convention)

**Disclosure means**: Written, oral, or by use
**Grace period**: Vietnam has a 6-month grace period for disclosure made by the inventor or with inventor's consent (Article 60.3) — but do NOT rely on this; file first is always safest.

### Article 61 — Inventive Step
Invention has inventive step if:
- It is not obvious to a person having ordinary skill in the relevant technical field
- Based on prior art disclosed before the filing date

**Utility Solution (Giải pháp hữu ích)**: Does NOT require inventive step — only novelty + industrial applicability. Faster to grant, shorter term (10 years).

### Article 62 — Industrial Applicability
The invention must be capable of:
- Serial/mass manufacturing or repeated use
- Producing consistent results

## Part 2: Filing and Prosecution at NOIP

### Filing Authority
**National Office of Intellectual Property (NOIP)** — Cục Sở hữu trí tuệ
Website: www.noip.gov.vn
IP Library: iplib.noip.gov.vn

### Application Requirements (Article 100)
A Vietnamese patent application must include:
1. **Request form** (Tờ khai) — Form SC-01
2. **Description** (Mô tả sáng chế) including:
   - Title of invention
   - Technical field
   - Background / prior art
   - Summary of invention
   - Detailed description
   - Best mode
3. **Claims** (Yêu cầu bảo hộ) — defines the scope of protection
4. **Abstract** (Tóm tắt) — max 150 words
5. **Drawings** (Bản vẽ) if necessary
6. **Priority document** (if claiming Paris Convention priority)
7. **Power of attorney** (if filed through an agent)

### Language
- Vietnamese is the official filing language
- Foreign-language documents must be translated

### Filing Fees (approximate, subject to change)
- Basic filing fee: VND 180,000 per independent claim
- Additional independent claims: VND 90,000 each
- Publication fee: VND 120,000
- Substantive examination request: VND 720,000 (must file within 42 months of filing date)

### Timeline — Vietnamese Patent Prosecution
| Stage | Timeframe |
|-------|-----------|
| Filing | Day 0 |
| Formality examination | 1 month |
| Publication in Official Gazette | 18 months from filing |
| Substantive examination (if requested) | 18–36 months after examination request |
| Grant | Grant date + 20 years protection |

**Key deadline**: Substantive examination must be requested within **42 months** of the filing date. If not requested, application lapses.

## Part 3: PCT in Vietnam

Vietnam is a PCT member state (since 1993).

### Filing route options:
1. **National filing only**: File directly at NOIP in Vietnamese
2. **Paris Convention**: File at home office → enter Vietnam within 12 months
3. **PCT**: File international application → enter Vietnam national phase within 31 months from priority date

### National Phase Entry in Vietnam
- Deadline: **31 months** from earliest priority date
- File Vietnamese translation of claims + description
- Pay national phase entry fees
- Designate Vietnam in the PCT application (VN)

## Part 4: 2022 Amendments (Law No. 07/2022/QH15)

Key changes effective January 1, 2023:
- Strengthened provisions against patent filing bad faith
- Clarified patentability of biotechnology inventions
- Improved border control enforcement for IP violations
- Extended grace period clarifications
- Introduced administrative penalties for IP infringement

## Key Definitions

- **Người nộp đơn**: Applicant
- **Chủ sở hữu bằng độc quyền**: Patent owner
- **Tác giả sáng chế**: Inventor
- **Yêu cầu bảo hộ**: Claims (literally: "protection request")
- **Mô tả sáng chế**: Patent description/specification
- **Ngày ưu tiên**: Priority date
- **Ngày nộp đơn**: Filing date
- **Bằng độc quyền sáng chế**: Patent certificate
- **Giải pháp hữu ích**: Utility solution (lower inventive step)
