# Patent Skills — IP Protection for Inventors & Entrepreneurs

A comprehensive patent and IP skills plugin for inventors, entrepreneurs, and startups — built from the perspective of a Vietnamese patent attorney with expertise in Vietnamese IP Law, PCT, USPTO, and EPO.

---

## Skills

| Skill | What It Does | Trigger When... |
|-------|-------------|----------------|
| `patent-research` | Prior art search, patentability assessment, freedom-to-operate | "Is this patentable?", "search for similar patents", "prior art" |
| `provisional-claims` | Draft provisional application to secure priority date quickly | "Secure my idea fast", "provisional patent", "before I launch" |
| `application-writer` | Write complete, filing-ready patent application | "Write my patent", "draft patent application", "full patent spec" |
| `patent-strategy` | Build IP portfolio strategy and filing roadmap | "IP strategy", "patent roadmap", "patent moat", "VC due diligence" |
| `claims-analyzer` | Analyze, critique, and strengthen existing patent claims | "Review my claims", "are my claims strong?", "broaden my claims" |
| `office-action` | Respond to patent office rejections (NOIP, USPTO, PCT) | "Got a rejection", "office action", "examiner rejected claims" |
| `patent-valuation` | Value IP assets, license patents, commercialize technology | "Patent worth?", "licensing deal", "IP due diligence", "royalty rate" |

## Jurisdictions Covered

- 🇻🇳 **Vietnam** — NOIP (National Office of Intellectual Property), Vietnamese IP Law 2005/2022
- 🌍 **PCT** — WIPO international filing system (30-month national phase strategy)
- 🇺🇸 **USA** — USPTO, US patent law (35 USC §§ 101-112)
- 🇪🇺 **Europe** — EPO (general guidance)
- 🌏 **ASEAN** — ASPEC cooperation, regional filing strategy

## Who This Is For

- **Inventors** who want to protect their inventions without needing to understand all the legal complexity
- **Entrepreneurs & Startups** who need to build patent portfolios that satisfy VC investors
- **R&D teams** who generate inventions and need a systematic way to capture, evaluate, and file them
- **Patent attorneys** who want AI assistance in drafting, research, and strategy

---

## Important Disclaimer

This plugin provides strategic guidance and drafting assistance. It does not constitute legal advice and does not establish an attorney-client relationship. For official filings, always have a licensed patent attorney in the relevant jurisdiction review the application before submission.

For Vietnam: The plugin's guidance is based on Luật Sở hữu trí tuệ (IP Law) as amended through 2022 (Law No. 07/2022/QH15). Always verify current NOIP requirements and fee schedules at www.noip.gov.vn.
